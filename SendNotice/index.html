Resident Check-In and Notification System

"Every great developer you know, got there break solving problems they were unqualified to solve." - Patrick McKenzie
Units To Be Notified Will Appear Below
This is a productivity app for businesses that would like to send a notification to their residents. The unit information is entered and stored for future notifications during the Check-In session. Then, the resident is notified, as needed. Additional units can be added before clicking "Notify" in order to send bulk notification. Every notification is logged with a date time stamp. The unit record is updated when the next resident checks in, or a "Check-out" feature can easily be added to the process.
